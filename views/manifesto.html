The bigCGI Manifesto

The mission of bigCGI is to assist in the solutions to these grievances:

  • The internet is bloated by an unnecessary and reckless use of javascript.
  • Ad networks do not respect users' privacy, usability, or intelligence.
  • Internet content should be independent of massive corporations.
  • The internet is becoming a consumerist wasteland, where information and content producers are becoming increasingly devalued.
  • The internet has a high barrier of entry for novices, students, and small business.

I am calling for a return to web minimalism and the end of corporate encouragement of content overconsumption at the expense of the end user and the greater good.
